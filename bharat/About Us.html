Established in 2000, My eyecare (Group of Eye Hospitals) is among the largest provider of quality eye care in Delhi-NCR. We take pride in offering the latest world-class technologies for eye care and ophthalmic treatments at very affordable cost. Our team of ophthalmologists are handpicked and some of the finest surgeons in the country.

Driven by the quest to protect and prevent vision loss and blindness,My eyecare eye hospitals have successfully conducted over 1,00,000 eye surgeries of people from all walks of life. Our chain of hospitals are a dedicated, quality-driven, modern surgical centres of excellence catering to patients from all corners of the country and abroad.

In the field of Ophthalmology,My eyecare has several firsts to its credit. We provide comprehensive eye care treatments ranging from routine eye tests to treatment of the most complex vision and eye-related problems like cataract, glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration, squint, etc.

Our hospitals are aesthetically designed with spacious and air conditioned In and Out-Patient Department'. They are fully equipped to handle all kinds of routine and emergency procedures. Our hospitals in Delhi-NCR are easily accessible; well connected with Metro Railways. These are designed for the convenience of disabled and elderly patients. Also, for the ease of patients/visitors we have in-house Optical Outlet, Pharmacy, Laboratory and well-kept Cafeteria.

My eyecare (Group of Eye Hospitals) has a network of seven centres across Delhi NCR - South/East/West/Central Delhi and Ghaziabad. Also, we have our eye centres in UP and Srinagar (Jammu & Kashmir).

Recognised as one of the best eye care service providers,My eyecare (Group of Eye Hospitals) is empanelled with leading health insurance companies and all major Govt. Of India Empanelments.

We regularly cater to patients from international countries like USA, Europe, Africa, Afghanistan, Iran, Iraq, and Australia, etc.
